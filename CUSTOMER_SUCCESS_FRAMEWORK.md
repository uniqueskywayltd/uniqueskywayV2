## Purpose

Define what “customer success” means on Unique Sky Way—so Milestone 6 builds a **Customer Success Platform**, not a marketing growth hack suite.

## Definition

A customer is succeeding when they can, over time:

1. **Locate** their money without anxiety.  
2. **Explain** statuses in ledger-honest language.  
3. **Act** (fund, invest, withdraw) without surprise.  
4. **Prove** history (statements / ledger) to themselves or advisors.  
5. **Share** the product accurately (referrals) without leaking privacy.  
6. **Self-serve** routine questions via Help and Education.  
7. **Return** months later and re-orient in seconds.

Wave B largely delivered (1)–(3). Milestone 6 deepens (4)–(7) without undoing Wave B calm.

## Success journeys

| Journey | Customer outcome | Milestone 6 contribution |
| --- | --- | --- |
| First 14 days | Understand empty/funded states; complete first deposit confidently | Education + Help deep links |
| Active investing | Track progress; know next NY settlement expectation | Insights (bounded) + education footnotes |
| Cash-out | Predictable withdrawal review language | Help + status education (no engine change) |
| Annual / tax | Obtain official statements | Statements |
| Advocacy | Invite peers honestly | Referral experience |
| Long-term loyalty | Feel recognized for real milestones | Quiet achievements |
| Support escalation | Get unblocked with facts | Success Center + Help |

## Success metrics (product intent — not vanity)

Prefer:

- Time-to-orient after return visit  
- Self-serve resolution (help/education) before tickets  
- Statement download completion without support  
- Referral share attempts with policy-correct copy  
- Zero critical money UX regressions vs `v3.1.0`  

Reject as primary goals:

- Daily streak length  
- Notification open rate via spam  
- Banner click-through on money home  

## Anti-patterns (never “success”)

| Anti-pattern | Why rejected |
| --- | --- |
| Fake urgency / countdown to invest | Undermines Trust → Confidence → Retention |
| Points for depositing | Turns finance into a game |
| Public leaderboards of earnings | Privacy + envy dynamics |
| Interrupting withdrawal with referral upsell | Money anxiety before marketing |
| Invented insights (“you’re crushing it”) | Dishonest if not ledger-backed |
| Duplicate money dashboards | Dilutes Wave B single home |
