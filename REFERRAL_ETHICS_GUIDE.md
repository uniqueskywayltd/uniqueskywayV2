## North star

> **How do I recommend this platform responsibly?**

Not “How do I maximize referral income?”

Referrals are invitations to a trusted service — not an affiliate hustle.

## Principles

### ETH-001: Invitation, not pressure

Share when it is a genuine recommendation. No nagging, no forced share walls on wallet/deposit/withdraw paths, no “you’re behind” comparisons.

### ETH-002: No spam or bulk messaging

The product must not encourage mass-invite blasts, scraped contact lists, or auto-messaging tools. Prefer one-to-one responsible sharing.

### ETH-003: No deceptive incentives

Share copy must not invent returns, guarantees, risk-free language, or hidden conditions. Align with Wave A public trust and glossary honesty.

### ETH-004: Privacy absolute

Never expose referred customers’ PII, balances, investments, or referral trees. Status labels only (pending / qualified / rewarded / voided) as policy allows.

### ETH-005: Reward participation, not aggression

Rewards acknowledge successful, eligible participation recorded by the frozen referral engine — never “invite faster” games, rank climbing, or volume contests.

### ETH-006: Honest eligibility

Explain when a reward is pending vs posted. Posted rewards are ledger money. Do not invent ETAs for ops-dependent qualification.

### ETH-007: Support responsible sharing

Provide calm guidance: how the code works, what not to claim, link to Learning / Help. Never coach customers to misrepresent Unique Sky Way.

### ETH-008: Brand continuity

Referral UX stays premium, quiet, and adult — same calm as Success Hub and Learning. No casino scarcity, countdown timers, or MLM aesthetics.

## Explicit bans

- Leaderboards and rank systems  
- Countdown / fake scarcity  
- MLM-style trees as social networks  
- Mass-invite tooling  
- Pressure upsells on money critical paths  
- Exposing other customers’ data  

## Relationship to engines

This guide does **not** change referral commission math, qualification, or posting. Financial truth remains `FINANCIAL_INVARIANTS.md` + frozen referral domain.
